About the System

Hostel Leave Management System

A modern web application designed to simplify hostel leave management through a secure, paperless, and efficient workflow.

For Students

Apply for hostel leave online, monitor application status, and access your complete leave history from one dashboard.

For Wardens

Review requests, approve or reject applications, and efficiently manage hostel leave records in real time.

Secure Platform

Separate login portals, secure authentication, and centralized data management ensure reliability and privacy.
